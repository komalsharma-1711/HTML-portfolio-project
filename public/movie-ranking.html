The Best K-Dramas According To Komal

My Top 3 K-Dramas Of All Time.

Lovely Runner

I really like the plot, the genre of the drama. It had everything comedy, romance, love, devotion, fantasy, thriller and suspense element.I really liked the casting and the chemistry between them .

"Thank you for staying alive ... If you keep this up, maybe a day will come when life won't seem so sad.
"Losing your heart sounds less painful then losing you forever again."
"Even if you were to end up in a different time, I would jump over time and space to see you."

Doom At Your Service

It had a very unique concept .It was so cute, slow paced and emotional kind of drama.

"You can't love me. No human has ever loved me before.Everyone either resents me or just want me"

The Sound Of Magic

the genre of this drama is slice of life.Now this drama is something I can't explain in words. The concept ,the casting ,the storyline ,the music , the emotions everything was top-notch. A 10/10 drama I watched in 2022 and still can't forget.This will be my one of the first recommendation to anyone new to kdrama.

"Annarasumanara" (Do you believe in Magic)
